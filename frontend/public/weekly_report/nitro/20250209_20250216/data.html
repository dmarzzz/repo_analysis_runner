OffchainLabs/nitro

Wartime Milady CEO Summary

  • - **Hex Encoding Bidder Signatures:** Two pull requests focused on hex encoding bidder signatures in CSV files published to S3, ensuring the signatures are no longer stored as raw bytes. This change is aimed at improving data readability for external parties, and it was implemented pre-mainnet, so existing files do not require modifications.
  • - **Metadata and Log Tracking Enhancements:** Several PRs concentrated on improving metadata tracking and log retrieval. Notably, enhancements were made to back-fill missing block metadata and to configure the tracking of metadata in Sepolia. Additionally, the `get_logs` call was optimized by processing in smaller chunks, addressing issues with large log queries.
  • - **Documentation and Maintenance Updates:** Various PRs were dedicated to enriching documentation, fixing links
  • Overall: 25 PRs closed, 19 contributors.
