Wartime Milady CEO Summary

  • - **EIP Updates and Moves to Last Call**: Several EIPs have been updated and moved to the "Last Call" stage, indicating readiness for final review. Notably, EIP-1898, EIP-7251, EIP-7002, EIP-7685, and EIP-6110 have all been transitioned, reflecting their maturity and community support.
  • - **ModExp Gas Cost Update**: A significant update was made with PR #9356, which proposes a modification to the `ModExp` pricing algorithm introduced in EIP-2565.
